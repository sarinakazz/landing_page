How do I cancel my subscrition?

Cancel your Premium plan any time on your account page.
1. Log into whimsic.com/account.
2. Under Your plan, click CHANGE PLAN.
3. Scroll to Whimsic Free and click CANCEL PREMIUM.
4. Your Premium stays until your next billing date, then your account switches to free.

You keep your playlists and saved music & visuals when your account is free. You can also still log in and play with ads.
Note: You can also cancel by completing this form and sending it to Spotify.
