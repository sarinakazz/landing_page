How can we help you?

Getting Started

Millions of songs and thousands of visuals are only a few steps away.

1. Get the app
Whimsic is available on desktop, mobile, tablet, TVs, cars, watches and more!

2. Create an account
Just choose SIGN UP from the login screen in the app. You can use your email address or phone number, or Facebook, Apple or Google accounts.
More sign up details are optional, such as gender, date of birth, and what we should call you (your display name).

3. Start Playing
Explore and discover! The more you use Whimsic, the more we get to know you and can recommend music and podcasts just for you. Pick your device for the basics of how to play.

How do I cancel my subscrition?

Cancel your Premium plan any time on your account page.

1. Log into whimsic.com/account.
2. Under Your plan, click CHANGE PLAN.
3. Scroll to Whimsic Free and click CANCEL PREMIUM.
4. Your Premium stays until your next billing date, then your account switches to free.

You keep your playlists and saved music & visuals when your account is free. You can also still log in and play with ads.
Note: You can also cancel by completing this form and sending it to Whimsic.
